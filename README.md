# 🔥 BurnSight AI – Early Workforce Risk Intelligence System

## Overview

BurnSight AI is an AI-powered workforce analytics platform designed to identify early signs of employee burnout and workforce risk.

Instead of simply predicting employee attrition, the system combines machine learning, anomaly detection, and simulation techniques to help organizations understand workforce well-being and evaluate interventions before productivity declines.

The project explores how artificial intelligence can support proactive workforce management and data-driven decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement

Organizations often recognize burnout only after it manifests through:

- Declining productivity
- Reduced job satisfaction
- Increased absenteeism
- Higher attrition rates
- Team performance issues

Reactive interventions are expensive and frequently come too late.

BurnSight AI aims to provide an early warning system capable of identifying workforce risk patterns and simulating possible improvements.

---

## Key Features

### 🎯 Burnout Risk Prediction
Predicts employee burnout risk levels:

- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk

---

### 📊 Workforce Capacity Index (WCI)
Generates a score between 0 and 100 representing overall employee well-being by considering:

- Work-Life Balance
- Job Satisfaction
- Environment Satisfaction
- Job Involvement
- Relationship Satisfaction

---

### 🧠 Behavioral Anomaly Detection
Uses unsupervised learning techniques to identify employees whose behavioral patterns differ significantly from the rest of the workforce.

Examples:

- Sudden drops in satisfaction
- Unusual workload patterns
- Income-performance mismatches
- Atypical engagement scores

---

### 🔮 Employee Digital Twin
Simulates interventions and estimates their impact on workforce health.

Examples:

- Improved work-life balance
- Better work environment
- Increased job satisfaction
- Reduced overtime

The simulator estimates future workforce capacity and projected gains.
